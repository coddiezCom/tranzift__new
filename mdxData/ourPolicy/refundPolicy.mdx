## Refund Eligibility

**Recharges and Bill Payments:**

- Refunds may be requested for recharges and bill payments under the following circumstances:
  - Payment was processed but the service was not provided.
  - Payment was processed erroneously (e.g., incorrect amount charged).
  - The service provider confirms a refund is due according to their policies.

**Gift Cards:**

- Refunds for gift card purchases are generally not provided after the gift card code or PIN has been revealed, or if the gift card has been activated or used.
- Refunds may be considered for gift cards that are damaged or incorrect upon delivery, subject to verification.

---

## Refund Request Process

- To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at support@tranzift.com. Provide the relevant transaction details, including the transaction ID or reference number, along with a clear explanation of the refund request.
- We will review your refund request within a reasonable timeframe and communicate with you regarding the status and any further information required.

---

## Refund Approval and Processing

- If your refund request is approved:

  - Recharges and Bill Payments: We will initiate a refund to your original payment method or offer an alternative resolution, as applicable.
  - Gift Cards: We will process a refund to your original payment method, or issue store credit, as applicable.

- The time it takes for a refund to be processed and reflect in your account may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution. We will provide an estimated timeframe when confirming the refund.

---

## Denied Refund Requests

- We reserve the right to deny refund requests under the following circumstances:
- The request does not meet the eligibility criteria stated in this Policy.
- The refund request is fraudulent or made in bad faith.
- The refund request violates our Terms and Conditions.

## Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our customer support team at [support@tranzift.com](mailto:support@tranzift.com)
